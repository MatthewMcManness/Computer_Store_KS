Customer Check-In

Complete all steps for each customer

1 Find customer account
1.1 If no account exists, create one
2 Create ticket
2.1 Title: Brand Type - Services Needed
2.2 Description: Full description of problem "Customer says: ..."
3 Create invoices
3.1 Invoice for prepay items (hardware)
3.2 Invoice for post-service payments (data transfer, drive clone, Windows upgrade/install)
4 Take payment for prepay invoice
4.1 Customer signs receipt
5 Have customer sign & initial ticket contract
6 Collect any items for drop-off
6.1 Tape ticket to items
7 Thank customer
8 Bring item to the incoming work shelf
