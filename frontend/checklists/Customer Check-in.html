Customer Check-In

Complete all steps for each customer

1 Greet Customer
Look up account by phone number.
If no account exists, create one using the New Customer Document
2 Check in
Create a Ticket
Title: Brand Type - Services Needed
Description: "Customer says: ..."
Be sure to mark whether or not their is a power cord
3 Create two invoices
Invoice 1: Pre-Service Payment Invoice
Everything EXEPT: Password Removal, OS Upgrade, Data Transfers, Drive Clones
Invoice 2: Post-Service Payment Invoice
Password Removal, OS Upgrade, Data Transfers, Drive Clones
4 Checkout
Take Payment for Invoice 1
Make sure customer signs the receipt
Paper Ticket Creation
Have customer sign & initial ticket contract
5 End of Transaction
Thank the Customer and give them a farwell
Tape the ticket to the device
Place the ticket on the incoming shelf
